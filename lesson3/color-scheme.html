Color Scheme

The color scheme will follow colors of the rainbow. I want the page to be bright and happy. I want it to be attractive to children which will grab their attention.
The body of the background will be a light, sky blue. The header and footer will be white, like clouds. The fonts will be a combination of the colors of the rainbow which are represented in the logo. At this time I would like to use a different color for each of the links on the navigation page. The regualr paragraphs will be black to make reading easier.
