Noah's Forecast

Target Audience

Lucy Kooyman

Lucy is a 9 years old girl who is in 3rd grade. She likes all the colors of the rainbow but her favorite color is purple. She loves cats and has her own cat named Rose. Lucy enjoys nature and is interested in the weather. She wants to learn more about how the weather works. She has a purple tablet that she can use to check the website.

Elena Smith

Elena is a 6 year old girl who is in first grade. She adores rainbow unicorns and wants to get one some day. Elena is very independent and likes to dress herself. She wants to know what the weather is so that she can choose her clothes without her mommy's help. She has a rainbow tablet that she can use to check the website.

Scenarios

  1. What is the actual weather?
  2. What the weather feels like?
  3. What to wear today?
  4. How the weather works.
  5. Should I play inside or outside?
