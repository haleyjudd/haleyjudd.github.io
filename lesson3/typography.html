Typography

For this website I will be using two fonts selected from google fonts. The first font will be used for my headings and is called "Sigmar One." The second font will be used for any paragraphs. It is called "Love ya like a sister." Both of these fonts were selected because they bring a fun, youthful feeling to the page. They were also chosen because they compliment and contrast with one another.
